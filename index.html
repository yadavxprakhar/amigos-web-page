Who We Are

Driven by Youth, Focused on Grassroots Change

We empower dynamic youth networks to drive bottom-up, grassroots change directly inside underserved communities.

As a registered Section 8 non-profit, InAmigos Foundation acts as a catalyst for sustainable development—mobilizing passionate volunteers to bridge systemic gaps and deliver direct aid where it is needed most.

Our Core Philosophy

"Action at the grassroots level generates permanent impact. When passionate youth lead, entire communities rise."

What We Do

Our Flagship Initiatives

We operate 6 distinct projects focusing on human empowerment, animal rescue, and environmental sustainability.

Project Seva

Food Relief & Nutrition

Combating hunger through community kitchens, dry ration distribution, and daily hot meal drives for daily-wage laborers and underprivileged families.

Support this project

Project Bachpanshala

Child Education

Providing basic literacy, non-formal elementary education, and essential stationery/school kits to children living in slums and remote villages.

Support this project

Project Udaan

Women Empowerment

Conducting menstrual hygiene awareness camps, distributing sanitary products, and running vocational skill training centers for absolute self-reliance.

Support this project

Project Prakriti

Environment & Climate

Leading large-scale urban afforestation, seed ball creation, clean-up drives, and community-level environmental awareness campaigns.

Support this project

Project Jeev

Animal Welfare

Rescuing stray animals, facilitating veterinary care, hosting vaccination drives, and installing community water/food bowls for strays.

Support this project

Project Vikas

Community Development

Focusing on rural health camps, infrastructure support in remote schools, and disaster response mobilization during crises.
